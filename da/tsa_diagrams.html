Examples of Topical Structure Analysis Diagrams

Example Analysis 1

The editorial diagrammed below appeared in The New York Times of Dec. 9, 1998, Page A28, under the headline “The Election Commissioners’ Duty.”

Discourse topic = illegal fund-raising by Dole and Clinton
1. Federal Election Commission
2.  both candidates [ref=Clinton and Dole]
3.  both [ref=Clinton and Dole]
4.      the party
5.          ambiguity
6.              explicit language
7.                  the Democratic ads 
8.                      Mr. Clinton
9. they [the federal election commissioners]
10.                                 the laws
